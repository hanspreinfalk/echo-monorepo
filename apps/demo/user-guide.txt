# Hospital Management System — user guide

This is a **demo** of a hospital management app. Use the **left sidebar** to move between areas. **Forms open in pop-up windows** on top of the page.

**Important:** Nothing is saved permanently. Your changes only last until you **refresh or close the page**, then the sample data returns.

---

## Screen layout

- **Sidebar** — Choose which area to work in. You can collapse the sidebar with the arrow at the bottom so only icons show.
- **Top of the main area** — Shows which section you are in.

---

## Dashboard

This is the first screen you see. It shows overview numbers, charts, recent admissions, and today’s schedule. You **cannot add or edit** data here—use the other sections for that.

---

## Patients

1. Click **Patients** in the sidebar.
2. To **add a patient**, click **Add Patient**, enter first name, last name, date of birth, gender, and contact, then save.
3. Use **Search patients** to narrow the list by name or phone.
4. To **remove someone**, click the trash icon on their row and confirm.

---

## Appointments

1. Click **Appointments** in the sidebar.
2. Click **New Appointment**, pick patient, doctor, date, time, department, and visit type, then **Schedule**.
3. Use the search box to filter by patient, doctor, or department.
4. To delete an appointment, use the trash icon on the row and confirm.

---

## Medical Records

1. Click **Medical Records** in the sidebar.
2. Click **Add Record**, choose patient, record type, diagnosis or notes, and doctor, then **Save Record**. The date is filled in for you as today.
3. To delete a record, use the trash icon and confirm.

---

## Billing

1. Click **Billing** in the sidebar.
2. The summary numbers at the top are for display in this demo.
3. Click **New Invoice**, choose a patient, enter the service and amount, then **Create Invoice**. New invoices show as **Pending**.
4. To delete an invoice, use the trash icon and confirm.

---

## Inventory

1. Click **Inventory** in the sidebar.
2. Click **Add Item** and enter name, category, how many, unit (e.g. box), and expiry if needed. Small quantities may show as **Low Stock**.
3. To remove an item, use the trash icon and confirm.

---

## Staff

1. Click **Staff** in the sidebar.
2. Click **Add Staff**, enter name, role, department, and contact, then save. Join date and status are set automatically for new people in this demo.
3. To remove someone from the list, use the trash icon and confirm.

---

## Departments

1. Click **Departments** in the sidebar.
2. Each card shows the department, who leads it, beds and occupancy where relevant, and how many staff are listed for that department. You **cannot add or edit** departments in this demo.
